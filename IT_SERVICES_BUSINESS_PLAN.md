## Executive Summary

**Innovative Information Technology Resolutions** is a B2B technology services company specializing in software development, managed IT services, cybersecurity, AI/ML consulting, and blockchain solutions. We provide enterprise-grade solutions for mid-market and enterprise clients seeking to modernize their infrastructure, enhance security posture, and leverage emerging technologies.

**Target Market:** Mid-market companies (50-500 employees) and enterprises seeking specialized IT expertise  
**Revenue Model:** Subscription services, project-based work, product licensing, and consulting
